## <a name="filtering-data"></a><span data-ttu-id="e1c79-135">Filtrado de datos</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1c79-135">Filtering Data</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e1c79-p106">Probablemente en alguna ocasión quiera buscar en un subconjunto de registros únicamente (por ejemplo, solo las sesiones punto a punto frente a las sesiones punto a punto y las sesiones de conferencia juntas). Likewise, there will be times when you need to reduce the number of records that are returned. By default, a report can only display the first 1,000 records in a data set. To address these issues, most reports include a number of filtering options. For example, if you want to view only records for the time period January 1, 2011 through January 15, 2011, you can enter January 1, 2011 in the **From** box and January 15, 2011 in the **To** box.
