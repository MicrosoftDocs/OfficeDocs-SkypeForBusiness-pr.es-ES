## <a name="eavesdropping-sniffing-snooping"></a><span data-ttu-id="5dac4-122">Interceptación (rastreo o espionaje)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5dac4-122">Eavesdropping (Sniffing, Snooping)</span></span>
